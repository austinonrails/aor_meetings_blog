 **Did I mention that the first 100 drinks are on us?!!**

## When and where
 Bullalo Billiards  
 Corner of 6th and Brazos ( [Google Map](http://maps.google.com/maps?q=201+E+6th+Street,+Austin,+TX+78701&ll=30.268445,-97.741413&spn=0.015678,0.037851))
